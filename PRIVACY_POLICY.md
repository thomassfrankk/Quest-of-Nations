# Privacy Policy for Quest of Nations

**Effective Date:** January 2, 2026

## Overview

Quest of Nations is a simple geography quiz game. We do not collect, use, or store any personal user information.

## What We Don't Do

- We do not collect personal data
- We do not track users
- We do not store user information on servers
- We do not use analytics

## Local Storage Only

All game data (high scores and settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never sent to us or any external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses:
- **Google Mobile Ads** (in free version) - for displaying advertisements
- **StoreKit** - for in-app purchases (premium version)

These services may collect their own data according to their privacy policies.
