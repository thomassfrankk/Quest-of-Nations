# Privacy Policy for Quest of Nations

**Effective Date:** January 2, 2026

## Overview

Quest of Nations ("we," "us," "our," or the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly
- **Game Progress & High Scores:** Stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- **User Preferences:** Sound settings, haptic feedback preferences, and display mode preferences stored locally

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifier (for ad targeting)
- **Usage Data:** Game performance, gameplay analytics, and crash reports
- **Ad Data:** Information related to ad serving and performance through Google Mobile Ads

### 3. Third-Party Services

#### Google Mobile Ads
- We use Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements in the free version
- Google may collect identifiers for ad targeting purposes
- See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### StoreKit (Apple In-App Purchases)
- Apple processes in-app purchase transactions
- See Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

## How We Use Your Information

- **To provide and improve the App** - Game functionality, high score tracking, settings management
- **To display advertisements** - Serving relevant ads to non-premium users
- **To process in-app purchases** - Premium subscription management
- **To analyze usage patterns** - Understanding how users interact with the App
- **To maintain app security** - Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity

## Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage:** All game data (high scores, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device
- **No Remote Servers:** We do not store your personal game data on external servers
- **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data by uninstalling the App
- Opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings
- Disable location services if requested

## Children's Privacy

Quest of Nations does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
